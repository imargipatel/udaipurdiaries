City Palace

City Palace

Location: Old City, Udaipur

History: Built in 1559 by Maharana Udai Singh II

Best Time: October to March

Entry Fee: ₹300 for adults

Timings: 9:30 AM - 5:30 PM

Lake Pichola

Lake Pichola

Location: Heart of Udaipur

History: Artificial lake built in 1362 AD

Best Time: Sunset boat rides

Boat Fee: ₹400 for adults

Timings: 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Jagdish Temple

Jagdish Temple

Location: Near City Palace

History: Built in 1651 by Maharana Jagat Singh

Best Time: Early morning prayers

Entry Fee: Free

Timings: 5:00 AM - 9:00 PM

Fateh Sagar Lake

Fateh Sagar Lake

Location: North of Lake Pichola

History: Built in 1678 by Maharana Jai Singh

Best Time: Evening sunset views

Boat Fee: ₹150 for adults

Timings: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Timings: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM
