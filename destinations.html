Explore Udaipur

Discover the royal heritage and natural beauty of the City of Lakes

Explore Udaipur Destinations

City Palace

City Palace

Location: Old City, Udaipur

History: Built in 1559 by Maharana Udai Singh II

Highlights: Stunning architecture & Lake Pichola views

Entry Fee: ₹300 for adults

Timings: 9:30 AM - 5:30 PM

Lake Pichola

Lake Pichola

Location: Heart of Udaipur

History: Artificial lake built in 1362 AD

Highlights: Iconic lake with boat rides & palace views

Boat Fee: ₹400 for adults

Timings: 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Fateh Sagar Lake

Fateh Sagar Lake

Location: North of Lake Pichola

History: Built in 1678 by Maharana Jai Singh

Highlights: Scenic artificial lake with hills & cafes

Boat Fee: ₹150 for adults

Timings: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Jag Mandir

Jag Mandir

Location: Island on Lake Pichola

History: Built in 1551 by Maharana Karan Singh

Highlights: Beautiful island palace on Lake Pichola

Boat Fee: ₹400 (includes palace visit)

Timings: 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Jagdish Temple

Jagdish Temple

Location: Near City Palace

History: Built in 1651 by Maharana Jagat Singh

Highlights: Magnificent Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu

Entry Fee: Free

Timings: 5:00 AM - 9:00 PM

Saheliyon ki Bari

Saheliyon ki Bari

Location: North of Fateh Sagar Lake

History: Built in 18th century for royal ladies

Highlights: Garden of Maidens with fountains & marble elephants

Entry Fee: ₹10 for adults

Timings: 8:00 AM - 7:00 PM

Bagore ki Haveli

Bagore ki Haveli

Location: Gangaur Ghat, Lake Pichola

History: Built in 18th century by Amarchand Badwa

Highlights: Historic haveli with cultural performances & museum

Entry Fee: ₹100 for adults

Timings: 9:30 AM - 5:30 PM

Monsoon Palace

Monsoon Palace

Location: Sajjangarh Hills, Udaipur

History: Built in 1884 by Maharana Sajjan Singh

Highlights: Panoramic views of Udaipur & lakes at sunset

Entry Fee: ₹100 for adults

Timings: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Vintage Car Museum

Vintage Car Museum

Location: Garden Hotel, Udaipur

History: Collection of royal cars from Mewar dynasty

Highlights: Showcases royal cars & historical vehicles

Entry Fee: ₹300 for adults

Timings: 9:00 AM - 9:00 PM

Doodh Talai Lake

Doodh Talai Lake

Location: Near Lake Pichola

History: Small lake with ropeway to Karni Mata Temple

Highlights: Great sunset views & cable car ride to temple

Ropeway Fee: ₹200 for adults

Timings: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Gulab Bagh & Zoo

Gulab Bagh & Zoo

Location: Near Fateh Sagar Lake

History: Established in 1887 by Maharana Sajjan Singh

Highlights: Large garden with small zoo & library

Entry Fee: ₹20 for adults

Timings: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Ahar Cenotaphs

Ahar Cenotaphs

Location: Ahar, 2 km from Udaipur

History: Royal cremation ground of Mewar rulers

Highlights: Beautifully carved marble domes & memorials

Entry Fee: ₹30 for adults

Timings: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Eklingji Temple

Eklingji Temple

Location: 22 km from Udaipur

History: Famous Shiva temple, patron deity of Mewar

Highlights: Famous Shiva temple with intricate architecture

Entry Fee: Free

Timings: 4:30 AM - 7:00 PM

Ambrai Ghat

Ambrai Ghat

Location: Lake Pichola waterfront

History: Historic ghat with stunning lake views

Highlights: Peaceful spot perfect for photography & evening views

Entry Fee: Free

Timings: 24 hours (best at sunset)

Hathi Pol Bazaar

Hathi Pol Bazaar

Location: Old City, near City Palace

Famous for: Traditional Rajasthani paintings (Pichwai & Phad art)

Highlights: Handicrafts, colorful fabrics & souvenirs

Why visit: Best place for authentic local art & bargain shopping

Tip: Most shops run by local artisans - reasonable prices

Bada Bazaar

Bada Bazaar

Location: Old City, Udaipur

Famous for: Bandhani & Batik printed sarees, silver jewelry

Highlights: Mojris, camel leather bags & ethnic wear

Why visit: Oldest & busiest market in Udaipur

Tip: Best place to buy ethnic wear & silver ornaments

Chetak Circle

Chetak Circle

Location: Near Chetak Circle, Udaipur

Famous for: Decorative items, puppets & brassware

Highlights: Wooden crafts, pottery & miniature paintings

Why visit: Perfect for souvenir shopping

Nearby: Rajasthali Emporium with fixed fair prices

Bapu Bazaar

Bapu Bazaar

Location: Old City, Udaipur

Famous for: Clothes, textiles & gadgets

Highlights: Stationery, household goods & local items

Why visit: More local-style bazaar with mix of everything

Tip: Great for Udaipur T-shirts & comfortable cotton wear

Moti Chowk Bazaar

Moti Chowk Bazaar

Location: Near Jagdish Temple

Famous for: Handicrafts & traditional jewelry

Highlights: Antiques, miniature paintings & art shops

Why visit: Picturesque streets full of art shops

Tip: Don't miss hand-painted souvenirs & marble carvings

Shilpgram

Shilpgram

Location: Udaipur outskirts

Famous for: Rural & tribal handicrafts

Highlights: Handwoven fabrics, pottery & cultural performances

Why visit: Cultural village showcasing art from across Rajasthan

Tip: Visit during Shilpgram Festival (December) for best experience
